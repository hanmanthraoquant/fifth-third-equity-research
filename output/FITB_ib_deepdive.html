2 · Comerica accretion / dilution & TBV earnback

The signature all-stock-deal build. FITB issued ~245M new shares (27% of the pro-forma 906M). Day-1, before synergies, the deal is -5.1% to EPS (dilutive) — normal for a premium all-stock deal — but as the $850M cost synergies phase in it turns solidly accretive:

IB DEEP-DIVE CONCLUSION
Four independent lenses converge on "fairly valued with optionality": comps ~in line, SOTP a touch below (market pricing synergies), and the Comerica deal dilutive day-1 but ~14% accretive by 2027E with a sub-3-yr TBV earnback. The re-rating case rests on management delivering the $850M synergies — which is exactly what the sensitivity table prices.
